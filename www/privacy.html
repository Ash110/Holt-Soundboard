  •  How much of my data are you storing?
    None at all. This might come as a bit of a shocker, but we really don't want your data.
  •  Why are you not collecting any data?
    There are multiple reasons to not be collecting your data
    1. We don't really care much for your personal information.
    2. Collecting data requires setting up a secret server and sending data, which we have no idea how to do.
  •  Is the app GDPR compliant?
    Let's not kid ourselves by acting like either of us understands the GDPR guidelines. Also, since no data is being collected, GDPR does not apply. Cool Question.
  •  Is the app totally ad and tracking free?
    Absolutely! In fact, the app will be ad free forever, and no trackers will be added. The app is open sourced on Github, so you can check for yourself.
