FAQ menu
  •  How much of my data are you storing?
    None at all. This might come as a bit of a shocker, but I really don't want your data.
  •  Why are you not collecting any data?
    There are multiple reasons to not be collecting your data
    1. I don't really care much for your personal information.
    2. Collecting data requires setting up a secret server and sending data, which I have no idea how to do.
  •  Is the app GDPR compliant?
    Let's not kid ourselves by acting like either of us understands the GDPR guidelines. Also, since no data is being collected, GDPR does not apply. Cool Question.
  •  How does the app make money?
    Currently, it doesn't make any money at all. You can support development through PayPal on the Support Development tab in the side navigation bar.
  •  Do you own the audio tunes?
    I neither own, nor claim to own any of the sounds used in the app. All credits to FOX Media and NBC.
  •  Will you make a Jake or Gina or ... soundboard app?
    There are no plans at the moment. Designing a new app takes a lot of time, which I currently can't spare. However, if you're willing to make one, I'll be glad to assist.
  •  Who let the dogs out?
    Hitchcock and Scully.
  •  Can I share the audio tunes on Whatsapp/ other apps?
    This is coming in a future update. At the moment, you can write a mail to me with the audio tune you want and I'll be glad to send it to you.
  •  How do I set them as notification tones/ ringtones?
    You currently cannot. Maybe in a future update.
